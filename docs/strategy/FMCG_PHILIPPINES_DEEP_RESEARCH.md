### Consumer Mindset (2026)

Filipino consumers have evolved through three phases:
1. **Pandemic caution** (2020–2021)
2. **Revenge spending** (2022–2023)
3. **Choice fatigue** (2024–2026) — overwhelmed by options, skeptical of brands, protective of time/money/data

Brands must now **earn attention and trust** — presence alone is insufficient.

## 2. Platform Breakdown

### Facebook — Still Dominant
- 90M+ accounts, 94.7% internet user penetration
- Primary platform for news, shopping, community groups
- Critical for reaching provincial / non-Metro Manila audiences
- Facebook Marketplace and Groups drive discovery for sari-sari store owners

### TikTok — Growth Engine
- 62.3M users (53.6% of total population, 64% of internet users)
- 40 hours/month average time spent
- 1.73% median engagement rate (vs. Facebook 0.046%, Instagram 0.36%)
- TikTok Shop blurring content → conversion line
- Ad reach grew 27% YoY (+13.3M users)
- 57.9% female / 42.1% male audience split
- **FMCG food engagement rate: 5.02%** — highest across verticals

### Instagram — Aspiration + Authenticity
- Skews younger, urban (Metro Manila, Cebu, Davao)
- Reels now 38% of brand posts (surpassed images)
- Collaborative posts see **3.4x engagement boost**
- Micro-influencer partnerships outperform celebrity in cost-per-engagement

### YouTube — Research & Reviews
- Indispensable for product research phase
- Long-form reviews, demos, unboxing drive consideration
- YouTube Shorts growing for discovery

## 4. The Sari-Sari Store Ecosystem

> 1.3 million mom-and-pop stores handle ~70% of manufactured goods sold in PH.

- "Sari-sari" means "variety" in Tagalog
- ~75% women-owned
- Core to Filipino community — not just retail, but social hubs
- **"Tingi" culture** — single-serve sachets for budget-conscious consumers

### Digital Transformation
- **Packworks** — AI-powered tools for sari-sari stores; lifted sales 46% in 2 weeks
- **Growsari** — B2B marketplace connecting stores to FMCG distributors
- **Peddlr** — Point-of-sale and inventory management for micro-retailers

### FMCG Opportunity
Connect brand campaigns to the sari-sari channel:
- UGC from store owners showing product displays
- Micro-influencer content featuring tingi/sachet format products
- TikTok Shop → sari-sari store cross-promotion

## 5. UGC & Influencer Strategy for PH FMCG

### Why UGC Works Exceptionally Well in PH
- **92% of Filipino consumers** trust word-of-mouth over any other advertising
- UGC is 9.8x more impactful than influencer content for purchase decisions
- UGC-based ads get 4x higher CTR than traditional ads
- Filipino creators drive disproportionate engagement vs. similar-sized markets

### Recommended UGC Playbook

#### Tier 1: Nano/Micro Creators (1K–50K followers)
- Sari-sari store owners showing product in real context
- Home cooks using FMCG ingredients (Lucky Me hacks, Mama Sita's recipes)
- "Tingi haul" content — budget-friendly sachet reviews
- Merienda (snack time) ritual content

#### Tier 2: Mid-Tier Creators (50K–500K)
- Recipe / cooking content (Filipino cuisine is inherently shareable)
- "Pang-masa" (for the masses) lifestyle content
- Shoppertainment livestreams on TikTok Shop
- Brand challenge participation with Filipino humor twist

#### Tier 3: Macro / Celebrity (500K+)
- Campaign launches and brand ambassadorship
- TV × TikTok cross-channel amplification
- Event-driven content (fiestas, holidays, back-to-school)

### Content Formats That Win

| Format | Platform | Why It Works in PH |
|--------|----------|-------------------|
| Recipe / cooking hack | TikTok, FB Reels | Filipino food culture is deeply social |
| Product "tingi" haul | TikTok | Relatable to budget-conscious audience |
| Unboxing / first taste | YouTube, TikTok | 72.3% use social to research products |
| Sari-sari store tour | TikTok, Facebook | Authentic, community-rooted |
| Comedy skit w/ product | TikTok, Reels | Humor is #1 engagement driver |
| Before/after (cleaning, beauty) | Reels, TikTok | Visual proof of product efficacy |
| Livestream shopping | TikTok Shop | Shoppertainment = PH sweet spot |
| Challenge / hashtag | TikTok | Filipino creators love participatory content |

## 10. Strategic Recommendations

### For a PH FMCG Social Media Manager:

1. **Lead with TikTok** — Highest engagement, fastest growth, direct commerce via TikTok Shop
2. **Don't abandon Facebook** — Still 90M+ users; essential for provincial reach and community groups
3. **Invest in UGC over polished ads** — 92% of Filipinos trust word-of-mouth; authenticity wins
4. **Tap sari-sari store owners as micro-influencers** — Authentic, community-trusted, massive reach
5. **Embrace tingi/sachet culture** — Budget-friendly content resonates with the mass market
6. **Plan around "Ber months"** — Sep–Dec is peak FMCG; start Christmas content in September
7. **Cross-channel amplification** — TV + TikTok campaigns deliver highest incremental lift
8. **Shoppertainment is the PH sweet spot** — Livestream selling on TikTok Shop is exploding
9. **Speed > perfection** — Filipino social rewards operational agility and cultural reactivity
10. **Build for mobile-first** — 87.6% of PH web traffic is mobile; vertical video is mandatory
